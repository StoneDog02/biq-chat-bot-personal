BODYiQ assistant — local preview

Dev preview only. The widget renders and is interactive, but sending a message needs ANTHROPIC_API_KEY + VOYAGE_API_KEY set in .env.local — until then you'll get a friendly error bubble. That's expected.
Launcher demo: the pill fades in bottom-right after ~1.5s. This preview sets a short peekTriggerSeconds (3s) and a peekMessage, so the peek card auto-surfaces a few seconds after load. Dismiss it (×) or ignore it (8s) to collapse back to the pill; click the pill or peek to expand. Peek gating uses sessionStorage — open a new tab (or clear session storage) to see it again.
